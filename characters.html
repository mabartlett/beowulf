Characters

Beowulf

The titular hero of the epic is a Geat thane who comes to aid the Danes in fighting the monster Grendel and later Grendel's mother. He returns to the Geats where becomes king and eventually dies fighting a dragon.

Hrothgar

Hrothgar is the king of the Danes who accepts the help of Beowulf in fighting the monster Grendel. He is the lord of the magnificent hall Heorot, where he and his retainers enjoy feasts and mead.

Grendel and His Mother

Grendel is a powerful monster descended from the biblical character Cain who attacks Heorot out of jealous rage. His mother avenges his death but is then defeated by Beowulf.

Wiglaf

Wiglaf is a loyal Geat thane who aids his king Beowulf the fight with the dragon. Beowulf dies in the fight, but if it were not for Wiglaf, the dragon would have continued his destruction.
